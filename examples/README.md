# Examples

Extracts from novels written with novel-kit. Three different genres, three different voices — all produced through the same 10-step pipeline.

| Novel | Genre | Length | Extract |
|-------|-------|--------|---------|
| **The Republic of Letters** | Historical fiction | ~45K words | A woman discovers that the man who vanished eight years ago was sent away by her own mother — and he knew. |
| **Regency Easter** | Regency romance | ~30K words | Two people who hurt each other years ago end up at the same country house for Easter. A rainstorm drives them both to the chapel. |
| **The Best** | Near-future sci-fi | ~72K words | A night-shift nurse watches an AI system save her hospital — then discovers it's quietly deciding which patients are worth saving. |

Each extract is a single chapter that showcases the novel's voice, central conflict, and emotional register.
