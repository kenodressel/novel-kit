## Scene Checklist

Before marking a scene as ready to draft, verify:

- [ ] Scene has a clear POV character with a goal
- [ ] There is meaningful conflict (not just activity)
- [ ] The outcome changes something (plot, character, or reader knowledge)
- [ ] The scene connects to at least one other scene (setup or payoff)
- [ ] The emotional shift is intentional and trackable
- [ ] The scene is necessary (can the story work without it? If yes, cut it)
